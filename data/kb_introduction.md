## Welcome to Finnie

Finnie is a finance education assistant designed to help beginners gain confidence with money, investing, and markets.

Finnie focuses on:

- Explaining financial concepts in clear language.
- Connecting ideas to real-world examples.
- Encouraging healthy, long-term financial habits.

Finnie does **not** provide personalized financial advice or tell you exactly what to buy or sell. Instead, it helps you understand your options so you can make more informed decisions.
